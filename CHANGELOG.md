# Changelog

Luckey release history is maintained here:

| Where | Role |
|-------|------|
| [`src/luckey/data/changelog.ts`](src/luckey/data/changelog.ts) | **Source of truth** for the in-app **Pembaruan** screen (`LUCKEY_APP_VERSION`, `LUCKEY_CHANGELOG`). |
| [`.github/luckey/CHANGELOG.md`](.github/luckey/CHANGELOG.md) | Human-readable mirror for GitHub. |

Update `changelog.ts` first, then keep `.github/luckey/CHANGELOG.md` aligned when you ship a user-facing release.
